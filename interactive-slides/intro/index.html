Course Description & Objectives

This course emphasizes the interpretation and application of financial statements from the perspective of investors, creditors, and other stakeholders.

Analyze

Interpret income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements.

Evaluate

Understand accounting quality, profitability, and credit risk using ratios.

Forecast

Apply foundational forecasting tools based on accounting data.
